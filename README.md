# 🚀 TransparentFund  
### AI + Blockchain Powered Transparent Crowdfunding Platform for Verified NGOs  

---

# 🌍 Problem Statement

Millions of donors hesitate to contribute online because they **don’t know where their money goes**.

Traditional donation systems suffer from:

❌ Fake NGOs  
❌ Misuse of funds  
❌ Zero transparency  
❌ No milestone accountability  
❌ Weak donor trust  

---

# 💡 Our Solution — TransparentFund

TransparentFund is a next-generation crowdfunding platform where:

✅ Only AI-verified NGOs can register  
✅ Donors track every rupee donated  
✅ Funds release milestone-by-milestone  
✅ Public transparency ledger builds trust  
✅ Blockchain records create accountability  
✅ AI prevents fraud & fake documents  

---

# 🔥 Key Features

## 🧠 AI-Powered NGO Verification

Organizations must upload legal documents.

Our AI engine checks:

- Document authenticity  
- AI-generated fake files  
- Field mismatch detection  
- Registration legitimacy  
- Fraud signals  
- Confidence score system  

### AI Engine Priority:

1. **Gemini API** (Primary)  
2. **Claude API** (Fallback)

---

## 💸 Smart Donation System

Users can donate via:

- UPI  
- Card  
- Net Banking  
- Razorpay Checkout  

After payment:

✅ Receipt generated  
✅ Campaign updated live  
✅ Ledger updated instantly  

---

## 🔐 Milestone-Based Fund Release

NGOs receive funds only after submitting proof.

Example:

- Milestone 1: Build water pipeline  
- Milestone 2: Upload progress proof  
- Admin verifies proof  
- Then only funds released  

---

## 📜 Public Transparency Ledger

Every major activity is visible:

- Donations  
- Milestone approvals  
- Refunds  
- Fund releases  
- Proof submissions  

Trust through transparency.

---

## ⛓️ Blockchain Integration

Immutable trust layer used for:

- Donation logs  
- Milestone releases  
- Ledger hashes  
- Auditability
